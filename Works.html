How It Works!

A travel company plans surprise trips by customizing itineraries based on client preferences, maintaining strict confidentiality, and delivering a memorable reveal and experience. Continuous improvement and client satisfaction drive the company's success.

STEP 1

Fill out the quick survey. Let us know about your travel dates, preferences, budget and some more details

STEP 2

Get a call from our team for budget confirmation. We will also ask some specific details to make your vacation a happy and hassle free experience.

STEP 3

Relax while we get our travel experts to work tirelessly to prepare the perfect itinerary of your surprise destination based on your preferences. We do the work, research and add the surprise element to your journey.

STEP 4

We send you a teaser email to get you excited. The mail contains details like what clothes to carry, weather conditions and the departure information.

STEP 5

A day before your departure, you will receive the envelope which would contain all your booking vouchers, travel itinerary and other important details about your surprise destination.

STEP 6

It's time to explore!
